FAQ

Common questions on setup

Will this work for my specific trade?

Yes. ContractorOS templates are built with standard data fields. We configure specific picklists, tax codes, and terminology specifically for your trade during installation—whether you do roofing, plumbing, HVAC, electrical, or general contracting.

What software tools do I need to run this?

The standard stack operates on Airtable (for the base database), Cal.com (for online booking), Twilio (for crew/client text messages), n8n or Make (for the automation hubs), and QuickBooks Online (for financial sync). We help you set up accounts under your own control.

Do I or my crew need a software background?

Not at all. The core design philosophy is "zero-training required for crews." The field team interacts only with simple, mobile-friendly forms, while clients view standard PDF documents and sign links. Your office handles approvals inside a clean Airtable board.

How fast can we go live?

Once we have credentials and access, the base template installation is ready in 24–48 hours. Tailoring forms, setting up your logo/branding, and running end-to-end sandbox tests with your crew typically takes between 7 to 10 business days.
